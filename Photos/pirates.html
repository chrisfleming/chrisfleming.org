Pirates?

19 Sep 2007 • Photos

So I just happened to upload this picture on to my flickr pages in the early hours of the morning, not because of talk like a pirate day, but as part of my general sorting out of my photos taken over the Summer. However because of the pirate theme, it has made it's way onto the "Interesting" page on flickr!

We had stopped at Melrose Abbey on the way back from Katherine and Colin's wedding, and I took this picture while walking about on a fine sunny day.
