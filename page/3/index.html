Sunset Over the Forth and Union Canal

09 Mar 2007 • Photos

Sunset over the Forth and Union Canal This was taken from the Lemmington Lift Bridge, looking West. I caught the sun setting over the canal, I love the contrast of angular lines of the soon to be demolished brewery on the right and the raggard roof lines of the flats on the left.
